About Our Project

This project leverages OpenCV and AI/ML to provide real-time patient monitoring in a hospital setting. Our goal is to enhance patient safety and streamline hospital management by automating the detection of critical events like falls or signs of distress.

The system is designed to integrate with existing CCTV infrastructure, providing a non-intrusive way to monitor patients while respecting their privacy. Alerts are sent to a central dashboard, allowing hospital staff to respond promptly to emergencies.
