Our Core Monitoring Services

The Hospital Monitoring System is designed around three pillars of patient safety and operational efficiency, ensuring immediate, reliable response.

1. Real-Time Fall Detection (AI Powered)

This primary service uses a trained neural network to analyze live video feeds. It requires confirmation over multiple consecutive frames to trigger an alert, significantly minimizing false alarms and providing crucial time-to-respond metrics.

  • Immediate audio/visual alerts sent to the staff dashboard.
  • Low confidence prediction display for clinical team review.
  • Frame-based **persistence threshold** for confirmed emergencies.

2. Dynamic Video Stream Management

We provide a centralized dashboard for viewing multiple patient camera feeds simultaneously, utilizing Flask's efficient video streaming capabilities (using multipart/x-mixed-replace).

  • Centralized, multi-room dashboard view for quick monitoring.
  • **Time-limited streams** (currently 30s) to conserve network bandwidth and memory.
  • Secure, easy integration with standard hospital web infrastructure.

3. Alert Logging and Auditing

Every critical event is logged with a precise timestamp and room location. This creates a non-repudiable audit trail for hospital management to review response times and incident history, improving care quality.

  • **Timestamped log** of all 'Fall Detected' events.
  • Dedicated alert sidebar for easy visibility of recent incidents.
  • Emergency modal requires acknowledgment, ensuring staff accountability.
