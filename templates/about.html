About Our Project

This project leverages OpenCV and AI/ML to provide real-time patient monitoring in a hospital setting. Our goal is to enhance patient safety and streamline hospital management by automating the detection of critical events like falls or signs of distress.

The system is designed to integrate with existing CCTV infrastructure, providing a non-intrusive way to monitor patients while respecting their privacy. Alerts are sent to a central dashboard, allowing hospital staff to respond promptly to emergencies.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce preventable injuries in healthcare facilities by providing a reliable, passive monitoring solution. We aim to empower clinical staff with immediate, actionable data to improve patient outcomes and increase operational efficiency.

Technology Stack

The system is built on a robust, lightweight stack ideal for real-time processing:

  • **Backend:** Python, Flask, and Flask-SocketIO for real-time communication.
  • **AI/ML:** TensorFlow and Keras for the trained fall detection model.
  • **Video Processing:** OpenCV (cv2) for frame capture and manipulation.
  • **Frontend:** HTML5, CSS (Styled), and JavaScript for a responsive dashboard.
